Recycle, Resist evaporation

We offer a solution to prevent drinking water pools from evaporating. We reduce the evaporation of water through black balls (balloons) that we recyclably collect and place on the surface of the water.

why choose us?

nnovative Approach: Our solution prevents potable water pools from evaporating with an innovative approach. It is a more effective and sustainable solution compared to traditional methods.
Environmental Impact: Since the recyclable balls we use are environmentally friendly materials, they do not harm nature and offer a sustainable solution.

Cost-Effectiveness: Our solution reduces the maintenance costs of drinking water pools and provides long-term cost savings by preventing water loss.
Customizable Solutions: Each drinking water pool has different features. We aim to provide the best performance to our customers by offering specially designed solutions for each pool.
